Stuck in your head? Describe what's bothering you and get fresh perspectives from 20 AI personas — a comedian, a stoic philosopher, a therapist, your grandma, even your dog. All powered by on-device AI. Your thoughts never leave your iPhone.

WHAT IT DOES

Type out what's on your mind (20-50 words), then scroll through perspectives from wildly different viewpoints. A nihilist who finds freedom in meaninglessness. A five-year-old who suggests snacks. A drill sergeant with zero patience for overthinking. Each one engages with your specific situation — no generic advice.

The goal isn't to solve your problem. It's to break the loop. When you see your worry through 20 different lenses, the grip loosens.

COMPLETELY PRIVATE

This is not a cloud app. A custom AI model runs entirely on your iPhone. Your words are processed locally and never transmitted anywhere. No accounts. No sign-ups. No tracking. No analytics. No ads. The privacy label says "Data Not Collected" because we literally collect nothing.

The AI model downloads once (~2 GB) on first launch, then everything works offline.
